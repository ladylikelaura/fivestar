## SERVICES

**Eyebrow:** What We Do

**Headline:**
End-to-End Medical
Logistics Services

**Intro:**
From STAT specimen runs to scheduled pharmaceutical routes, Five Star handles the full spectrum of medical courier needs across the healthcare network. Specialized, compliant, and built for the pace healthcare demands.

---

### 🧪 Specimen Transport
Same-day pickup and delivery of blood draws, tissue samples, cultures, and all lab specimens. Temperature-sensitive handling with complete chain of custody documentation for labs, hospitals, and outpatient clinics.

### 💊 Pharmaceutical Delivery
Secure, time-critical delivery of medications from pharmacies to patients, facilities, and care centers. Proper handling protocols maintained throughout, with delivery confirmation on every run.

### 🏥 Hospital Supply Runs
Scheduled and on-demand delivery of medical supplies, equipment, and materials between hospitals, surgical centers, and outpatient facilities. Reliable routing, flexible scheduling.

### ⚡ STAT Emergency Pickup
When timing is critical and the standard courier won't do. Our STAT service dispatches immediately for urgent specimen pickups, critical supply deliveries, and time-sensitive medical transport.

### 🧬 Lab-to-Lab Logistics
Coordinated pickup and delivery between independent laboratories, reference labs, and diagnostic centers. We understand specimen integrity requirements and handle each transfer accordingly.

### 🏠 Home Health Support
Bridging home health agencies and their patients with the facilities that support them. Specimen collection pickups, medication delivery, and supply logistics for the growing home health network.
