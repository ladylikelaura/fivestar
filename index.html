What We Do

End-to-End Medical
Logistics Services

From STAT specimen runs to scheduled pharmaceutical routes, Five Star handles the full spectrum of medical courier needs — specialized, compliant, and built for the pace healthcare demands.

Specialized handling — every pickup, every delivery, every time
🧪
01
Specimen Transport

Same-day pickup and delivery of blood draws, tissue samples, cultures, and all lab specimens. Temperature-sensitive handling with complete chain of custody documentation.

💊
02
Pharmaceutical Delivery

Secure, time-critical delivery of medications from pharmacies to patients, facilities, and care centers. Proper handling protocols with delivery confirmation on every run.

🏥
03
Hospital Supply Runs

Scheduled and on-demand delivery of medical supplies, equipment, and materials between hospitals, surgical centers, and outpatient facilities.

⚡
04
STAT Emergency Pickup

When timing is critical and the standard courier won't do. Immediate dispatch for urgent specimen pickups, critical supply deliveries, and time-sensitive transport.

🧬
05
Lab-to-Lab Logistics

Coordinated pickup and delivery between independent laboratories, reference labs, and diagnostic centers. We understand specimen integrity requirements.

🏠
06
Home Health Support

Bridging home health agencies and their patients with the facilities that support them — specimen pickups, medication delivery, and supply logistics for the home health network.

Built for Healthcare's
Standards — Not Around Them
